Welcome to Wordle!

This verion of Wordle is similar to regular old Wordle, but with some fun twists. Everyday, there will be a new word to guess, and the length of this word will vary from 1-10 letters. Each letter in the word corresponds to the number of guesses you will have. For example, if the word is 'snowman', you will have 7 total guesses.
After completing the game, you will be taken to the leaderboard where you can see the top 5 users according to how fast they guessed the word. Keep in mind that only your first correct guess will be saved for every word.
If you have any thoughts you would like to share about the game, hit the feedback on the right side of the screen to leave a comment.
Thanks for playing!
